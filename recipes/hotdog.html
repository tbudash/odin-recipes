Homemade Hotdog

This recipe is perfect for cook out on summer days, or for a quick and easy lunch

Here's what you'll need:

  • Hot Dogs
  • Hot Dog Buns
  • Cheese
  • Pickles

Instructions

  1. To cook your hot dog, toss it in the microwave for 2 min
  2. Prepare a bun to place the hot dog on
  3. Put the dog in the bun and top with cheese and Pickles
