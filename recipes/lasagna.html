Lasagna

This is a filling Italian dish that is perfect for a nice dinner any time of the year

Here's what you'll need:

  • Lasagna pasta
  • Ricotta, Parmesan, and Mozzerlla cheese
  • Beef
  • Parsley
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Lasagna Pasta

Cooking Instructions

  1. Boil Lasagna Pasta
  2. Prepare the sauce, Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl
  4. Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until browned and bubbly
