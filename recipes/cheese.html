Grilled Cheese

This recipe is great for a quick snack and pairs well with tomatoe soup!

Here's what you'll need:

  • Loaf of bread
  • Sliced cheese of your liking
  • Butter

Cooking Instructions

  1. First, prepare a pan over the stove
  2. Next, butter one side of a piece of bread and place on pan
  3. Put a few slices of cheese ontop of the bread and top with a second slice of bread, also buttered on the top
  4. Flip after a few minuetes and cook the other side until done
